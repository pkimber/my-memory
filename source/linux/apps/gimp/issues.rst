Missing toolbar
===============

*Edit*, *Preferences*, *Window Management*:

- untick *Save window positions on exit*
- click *Reset Saved Window Positions to Default Values*

Restart GIMP

(From `how to fix missing toolbar in gimp 2.8`_)
