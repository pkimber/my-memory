Shortcuts
*********

+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *F5*              | Reload page including all static |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *K*               | Search bar.                      |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *L*               | Location bar.                    |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| ``/``                     | Search within the page.          |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Spacebar*                | Scroll                           |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+

Tab
===

+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *Shift*, *T*      | Re-open last closed tab.         |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, 1 ... 9           | Goto specific tab.               |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *w*               | Close current tab.               |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
| *Ctrl*, *t*               | Open new tab                     |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------+
